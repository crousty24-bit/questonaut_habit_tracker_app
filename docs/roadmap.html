Roadmap

A practical next-step view of the product.

Questonaut is already usable end to end as an MVP. The roadmap below separates what is already delivered from the next layers that would deepen retention, insight, and personalization.

Now

Shipped in the MVP

  • Authentication and personal dashboard
  • Daily and weekly mission management
  • XP, levels, badges, and login streaks
  • Statistics and category insights
  • Legal pages and cookie consent

Next

High-value product extensions

  • Richer mission templates for faster onboarding
  • More detailed progress insights and trend analysis
  • Optional reminder loops and recovery nudges
  • Sharper badge storytelling and milestone moments

Later

Broader product bets

  • Themed challenge packs
  • Premium analytics and personalization
  • Community or social accountability features
  • Content partnerships around self-improvement

Product Principle

Keep the main loop lightweight.

Future work should extend the feeling of momentum, not bury it. The core Questonaut experience works best when creation, validation, reward, and review remain fast to understand and easy to repeat.
